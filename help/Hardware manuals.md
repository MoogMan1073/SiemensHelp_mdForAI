## Introduction to hardware manuals

Using TIA Portal, you can configure a wide range of hardware. There are two ways of using the corresponding hardware manuals.

### Hardware manuals in the information system

You have the option of displaying hardware manuals as an integrated part of the information system. This has the advantage of allowing you to search the integrated contents, filter, or set as a favorite.

The TIA Administrator allows you to download hardware manuals that are available as support packages.

To do this, select the function "Check for updates" in the toolbar of the table of contents.

### Hardware manuals in the Siemens Industry Online Support

You can find all the current manuals, operating instructions and FAQs as well as updates for your devices in the Siemens Industry Online Support. To access the Online Support, you need an Internet connection.

[Siemens Industry Online Support](https://support.automation.siemens.com/)
