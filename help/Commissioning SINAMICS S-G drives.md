## Overview

**The default settings are valid for the following drive systems:**

The descriptions provided in this section are applicable for the following SINAMICS S drive systems:

- SINAMICS S120
- SINAMICS S150
- SINAMICS G150
- SINAMICS G130
- SINAMICS Integrated for SIMATIC Drive Controller

  (S120 for the CPU 150xD)

**Not valid for the following drive systems:**

For a series of SINAMICS drive systems, the operation of the SINAMICS S standard deviates, and is therefore explained in separate sections:

- [SINAMICS S210](Commissioning%20SINAMICS%20S210%20drives.md#commissioning-sinamics-s210-drives)
- [SINAMICS MV](Commissioning%20SINAMICS%20MV%20drives.md#commissioning-sinamics-mv-drives)
- [SINAMICS G120/G115D/G110M](Commissioning%20SINAMICS%20G120-G115D-G110M%20drives.md#commissioning-sinamics-g120g115dg110m-drives)

### Specifying the control type

#### Inserting drive axes for servo or vector control

You make the decision as to whether your drive axes are to operate with servo or vector control, when you insert a Motor Module or Power Module.

You can find additional information on this at [Changing the drive object type](Configuring%20SINAMICS%20S-G-MV%20drives.md#changing-the-drive-object-type).
